Frequently Asked Questions

Why is FlightTrace so much cheaper? +

We use modern serverless architecture and efficient data sources to keep costs low. We pass these savings directly to our users. Plus, we believe flight tracking should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford premium prices.

How accurate is the fuel and CO2 tracking? +

Our fuel consumption calculations use industry-standard formulas based on aircraft type, weight, altitude, and weather conditions. CO2 emissions are calculated using ICAO's official carbon calculator methodology. Accuracy is typically within 5-10% of actual consumption.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes! We offer true one-click cancellation from your account dashboard. You can cancel immediately with a pro-rated refund, or at the end of your billing period. We also offer the unique ability to pause your subscription if you need a break.

Do you have the same coverage as FlightRadar24? +

We use multiple data sources including OpenSky Network and ADS-B Exchange to provide comprehensive global coverage. While FlightRadar24 has more receivers, our coverage includes all major flight routes and airports worldwide.

Is there really no credit card required for the trial? +

Correct! Start your 14-day trial with just an email address. We only ask for payment information if you decide to continue after the trial ends. No surprises, no auto-charging.
